Homepage

The reference for the homepage was taken from the homepage of aspencore website. The website seemed minimalistic and eye-catching at the first look. The concept of keeping the color of navigation bar same as the welcome section looked pretty good. So, putting some own concepts and ideas and taking the color combination of the as top priority, the homepage was created. The homepage contains navigation bar through which whole page can be navigated thoroughly. It also contains some books displayed in sale, Topsellers and some more categories.

Products

The reference for the products page was taken from the bookshop.org website. The concept to keep sliding images on the products page seemed pretty good. So, by making a few amends and putting own ideas to lighten up the page, the products page was created. The products page contains navigation bar at the top and footer containing info at the bottom. The main content of products page is the books that the bookstore sells. The books are arranged into divisions according to their genre. There is also a recommendation section where a book is displayed with its short synopsis and another section where more books of same genre are displayed.

Blog

After going through a lot of blogging website, from simple to complex blog designs, and numerous hectic hours of choosing between designs, the reference for the blog page was taken from KnowTechie website. The design of the website seemed simple yet elegant and perfect for the blog. So, referencing with the website by adding some of own ideas and making some changes, the blog page was created. The blog is about how technology has affected our daily life with the heading "Imapcts of technology in daily life". Here, the positive and negative impacts of technolgy in our daily life are explained with adding images to the blog to make it look elegant with minimalist look. The blog page has a good color combination of shades of blue.

Contact Us

The wireframe of contact page was changed for the 6th time to make it look good. After visiting tons of websites and rewriting the code many times, the last viewed contact page was from website of fusemachines which was pretty eye-catching. So, the idea for contact page was referenced form fusemachines website. Since the webpage was good to look at, a lookalike webpage was created. The webpage contains a form div where javascript has been added for form validation.

Footer

The reference for the footer of the website has been taken from the footer of aspencore website. It has a neat look. As the look of the website is minimalist, the footer seemed perfect match. The footer has the links of facebook, instagram and twitter embedded in the images of the respective websites. The footer also has the contact info of the website. So, a similar footer was created.
